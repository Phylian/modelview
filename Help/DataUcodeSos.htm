UCODE-2005 Data Requirements

Data requirements for visualizing UCODE-2005 results are as follows:

  • Only data in _sos files is used.
  • No more than three parameters may be included in the _sos file.
  • The values assigned to each of the paramters must be listed in increasing order.
  • The values assigned to each of the paramters must be capable of being arranged in a regular grid.
